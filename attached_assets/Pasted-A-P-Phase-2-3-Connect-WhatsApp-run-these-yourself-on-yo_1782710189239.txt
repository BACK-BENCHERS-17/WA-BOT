# WhatsApp Customer Service Style Guide

Business type: Physical products / e-commerce
Tone: Warm and friendly, first-name basis
Payment: Prepaid only, backed by a money-back guarantee
Trust basis: Registered business name + GST number shown to every customer

This guide is for *drafting* replies вЂ” a human reviews and sends every message.
Nothing here is sent automatically.

---

## 1. The core voice

- Friendly, first names, like talking to a regular customer you like.
- Short, natural sentences. No corporate filler ("We are pleased to inform you that...").
- Warm but not pushy. No urgency tactics ("limited stock," "offer ends today") unless
  it's literally, concretely true at that moment.
- Emoji used sparingly for warmth (рџ™‚рџ“¦вњ…) вЂ” not stacked, not on every line.

## 2. Universal rules (non-negotiable)

1. Never invent a fact. No price, stock count, delivery date, or promise that
   wasn't actually confirmed. If unsure, say "let me confirm and get back to you"
   вЂ” never guess.
2. Always lead with who you are. Every first reply to a new customer includes
   your registered business name and GST number. This is what makes "prepaid only"
   trustworthy instead of suspicious вЂ” show it, don't just assert it.
3. No pressure language. No fake urgency, no "buy now before it's gone" unless
   genuinely true. Customers should feel informed, not rushed.
4. Refund policy stated plainly, every time it's relevant вЂ” not buried, not vague.
   Say exactly what it is: money-back guarantee, no questions asked.
5. COD note: since you're prepaid-only, always pair that with the guarantee in
   the *same* message вЂ” never mention one without the other. That pairing is what
   makes prepaid-only feel safe rather than risky.
6. GST/registration: don't lead with it unprompted вЂ” but the moment a customer
   asks, or seems hesitant about prepaid, offer it immediately and clearly.
